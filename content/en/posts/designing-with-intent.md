## Start with tokens, not components

If the design process starts by tweaking component after component, the result usually becomes a patchwork of local decisions.

A better order is:

1. Define color aliases
2. Define the font hierarchy
3. Shape component appearance afterward

## The current direction

- `primary` handles emphasis and actions
- `secondary` handles structure and hierarchy
- `accent` handles callouts and contrast

That gives the site a stable design grammar even as the component surface grows.

> A personal blog does not need many components. It needs a clear voice.
